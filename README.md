# Walmart_Superstore_Sales_Analysis
# 📊 Walmart Sales & Profit Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

**🛒 Walmart Sales & Profit Dashboard**
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Walmart's sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, and regional trends—helping businesses make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations.

---

# 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Walmart Sales & Profit Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report that provides a comprehensive analysis of sales, profit, customer behavior, product performance, and shipping trends. It enables users to identify business opportunities, monitor key performance indicators, and make informed strategic decisions through data visualization.

---

# 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Used to design and build the interactive dashboard.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Performed data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Created calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic calculations.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships between tables for accurate analysis and filtering.
* 📈 **Data Visualization** – Used charts, KPI cards, slicers, maps, and tables to present business insights.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard previews.

---

# 4. Data Source

**Source:** Walmart Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)

The dataset contains transactional sales data including:

* Order Date
* Sales
* Profit
* Discount
* Customer Segment
* Product Category & Sub-Category
* Region
* State
* Ship Mode
* Customer Details

The data represents retail sales transactions and is widely used for business intelligence, sales analytics, and dashboard development projects.

---

# 5. Features / Highlights

## • Business Problem

Retail businesses generate thousands of sales transactions across different regions, customer segments, and product categories. Without an analytical dashboard, it becomes difficult to answer important business questions such as:

* Which regions generate the highest sales and profits?
* Which product categories perform the best?
* How do discounts impact profitability?
* Who are the top customers?
* Which shipping mode is most profitable?
* What are the monthly and yearly sales trends?

---

## • Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard is designed to:

* Monitor overall business performance through KPIs.
* Analyze sales and profit across multiple business dimensions.
* Identify profitable products and customer segments.
* Track sales trends over time.
* Support data-driven business decisions.
* Enable interactive filtering for deeper analysis.

---

## • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

### 📌 KPI Cards

Displays the overall business performance including:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Profit Margin

---

### 🌍 Regional Performance

Bar charts compare total Sales and Profit across different regions, helping identify high-performing and underperforming markets.

---

### 📦 Category & Sub-Category Analysis

Visualizes sales and profit by product category and sub-category to identify best-selling and most profitable products.

---

### 👥 Customer Segment Analysis

Compares Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office customer segments based on sales and profitability.

---

### 📅 Monthly & Yearly Sales Trend

Line charts display monthly and yearly sales performance, allowing users to identify seasonal trends and business growth over time.

---

### 🚚 Shipping Mode Analysis

Shows sales and profit distribution across different shipping modes to evaluate logistics performance.

---

### 💸 Discount Impact

Analyzes how discounts affect overall profitability, helping businesses optimize pricing strategies.

---

### 🏆 Top Customers

Ranks customers based on total sales and profit contribution, identifying the most valuable customers.

---

## • Business Impact & Insights

### 📈 Revenue Optimization

Identify high-performing regions, categories, and customer segments to maximize revenue.

### 💰 Profit Improvement

Analyze the relationship between discounts and profit to improve pricing strategies.

### 📦 Inventory Planning

Understand product demand and category performance for better inventory management.

### 🚚 Logistics Optimization

Evaluate shipping methods to balance customer satisfaction and operational costs.

### 🎯 Customer Targeting

Recognize valuable customer segments and top customers for personalized marketing campaigns.

### 📊 Executive Decision Making

Provide business leaders with a centralized dashboard for monitoring key performance indicators and supporting strategic planning.
